About

The Creative Behind the Stage

Steven Mlazi is a creative professional specializing in stage design, lighting design, and instrumental performance.

His work combines creativity, technical expertise, and performance experience to deliver memorable experiences for audiences and clients. From intimate productions to large-scale live events, Steven brings vision, precision, and passion to every project.

Whether crafting atmospheric lighting, designing immersive stage environments, or delivering live instrumental performances, Steven approaches each engagement with the same commitment to excellence that defines his reputation in the industry.
